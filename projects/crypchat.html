CrypChat – UI/UX Case Study (2025)

Design exploration for a privacy‑focused messaging app. This is a UI/UX case study (design only), highlighting concepts like end‑to‑end encryption, ephemeral messages, and strong authentication patterns.

Figma UI/UX Prototyping

Case Study

The design explores flows for onboarding, secure messaging, and privacy controls. Wireframes progressed to high‑fidelity mockups with a clickable Figma prototype. No production code is included.

Highlights

  • Two‑factor authentication patterns in onboarding
  • Conversation list and chat screen with disappearing messages
  • Privacy settings with granular controls
  • Accessible color choices and contrast ratios

Tools

Designed in Figma; components organized with auto‑layout and variants to enable quick iterations.
