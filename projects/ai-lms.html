AI Learning Management System – UI/UX (2025)

Case study and interface designs for a role-based LMS featuring an AI assistant, content management, analytics dashboards, and automated quiz generation concepts. This is a UI/UX design, not a fully functional app.

Figma UI/UX Prototyping

Overview

This case study explores an LMS experience that streamlines course delivery and student engagement. The AI assistant concept helps answer questions, generates quizzes from content, and surfaces progress insights.

Key Features

  • Multi-role flows: Admin, Instructor, Student
  • AI assistant for Q&A and study guidance (concept)
  • Automated quiz generation from lesson material (concept)
  • Dashboard analytics: completion, scores, and activity
  • Content management with modules and lessons

Design Process

Wireframes → user flows → high-fidelity mockups in Figma with components, variants, and auto-layout. Prototyped key interactions for testing.
